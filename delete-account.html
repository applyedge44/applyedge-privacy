Delete Your ApplyEdge Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can request deletion of your ApplyEdge account and all associated data by following the steps below.

How to request account deletion:

  1. Send an email to applyedge44@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your ApplyEdge account
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days

What data will be deleted:

  • Your account information (name, email address)
  • Your usage history and credits
  • Any stored preferences

Data retention:

After account deletion, we may retain anonymised, aggregated data that cannot be linked back to you for up to 30 days for internal analytics purposes. All personally identifiable information will be permanently deleted.

For any questions, contact us at applyedge44@gmail.com
